Welcome to the new home of the Collaboratory for Physical Oceanography.

We rebuilt the lab website from the ground up as a static site hosted on
GitHub Pages. The entire site — content, design, and history — now lives in
a public repository at
[github.com/oceansensing](https://github.com/oceansensing), which makes it
fast, free to host, and easy for any lab member to update with a simple
commit.

Expect projects, publications, datasets, and news to grow here over time.
