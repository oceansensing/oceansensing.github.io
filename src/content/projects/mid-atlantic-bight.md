The Mid-Atlantic Bight is C4PO's home ocean. Since 2013 the lab has flown
autonomous underwater gliders across the shelf and shelf break, observing
seasonal stratification, cross-shelf exchange, storm response, and the
circulation within submarine canyons that incise the continental margin.

This work supports both process studies — including dissertation research
on submarine canyon dynamics — and regional observing efforts in
partnership with the broader Mid-Atlantic glider community.
