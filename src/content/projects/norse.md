The Northern Ocean Rapid Surface Evolution (NORSE) initiative investigates
how the upper ocean in the Nordic Seas evolves on short time scales —
where strong atmospheric forcing, sharp fronts, and energetic turbulence
interact at high latitudes.

C4PO contributes autonomous glider observations to the NORSE field
campaigns and studies mixing dynamics in the region, including graduate
research on turbulence and water-mass transformation in the Nordic Seas.
