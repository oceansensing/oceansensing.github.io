The Northern Ocean Rapid Surface Evolution (NORSE) initiative, an Office of
Naval Research departmental research initiative, investigates how the upper
ocean in the Nordic Seas evolves on short time scales — where strong
atmospheric forcing, sharp fronts, and energetic turbulence interact at
high latitudes. C4PO contributes autonomous glider observations to the
NORSE field campaigns near Jan Mayen, sailing aboard vessels including the
Norwegian icebreaker R/V *Kronprins Haakon*, and studies mixing dynamics
and water-mass transformation in the region — including Ricardo Bourdon's
doctoral research on turbulence in the Nordic Seas.
